Merchant Conduct & Rules

Agreement for Verified Partners

Terms of Service

By accessing this dashboard, you acknowledge that you have already agreed to the RUNHub Terms & Conditions during your physical verification and sign-up.

Platform Fee Remittance

You are a custodian of the platform fee. For every order (Approved or Declined), ₦50 belongs to RUNHub.

Failure to remit fees once your wallet reaches ₦500 will result in an automatic account lock.

Refund Policy

If you decline an order, you MUST refund the customer manually within 30 minutes.

You must refund the exact amount specified on the refund modal. Do not leave customers waiting; always Approve or Decline promptly.

Performance & Rating

Integrity is key. If your merchant rating drops below 2.0 Stars, your account will be automatically blocked and deleted.

Always remember to click "Completed" in the receipt of your Pending orders once delivery is finished to move the order to History.
