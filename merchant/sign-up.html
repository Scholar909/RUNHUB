STEP 1

ID Card Front

Place the FRONT of your ID inside the frame and take a photo.

STEP 2

ID Card Back

Flip the card and capture the BACK side.

STEP 3

Selfie Holding ID

Take a selfie while holding your ID clearly under your face. This will be used as your Profile Photo and will be displayed to your customers

STEP 4

Facial Scan

Position your face in the frame and stay still. Auto capture will occur in 5 seconds. This image will be used as passport for the Binding Agreement
