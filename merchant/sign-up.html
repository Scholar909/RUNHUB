Merchant Verification

Complete identity verification to become a RUNHUB merchant.

INTRODUCTION

You will complete identity verification before becoming a merchant.

  • Your ID card is available
  • You are in a well-lit environment
  • Your camera is working
STEP 1

ID Card Front

Place the FRONT of your ID inside the frame and take a photo.

STEP 2

ID Card Back

Flip the card and capture the BACK side.

STEP 3

Selfie Holding ID

Take a selfie while holding your ID clearly beside your face.

STEP 4

Facial Scan

Position your face in the frame and stay still. Auto capture will occur in 5 seconds.

STEP 5

Verification Video
