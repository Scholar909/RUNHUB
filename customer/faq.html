Safety & FAQ

Your guide to ordering and staying safe on RUNHUB.

How to Place an Order

  • Pick a merchant from your dashboard (ensure the slots left is large).
  • Customize your order in the popup modal—adjust items and food packs.
  • Review the breakdown. Click Pay & Confirm after transferring to the merchant's bank details.
  • Transfer the exact total shown. Once done, click "Pay & Confirm".

Important Billing Rules

  • Platform Fee: A fixed ₦50 fee is applied to every order.
  • Note: The ₦50 platform fee is non-refundable. Even if a merchant declines your order, the platform fee is retained for system processing.
  • Refunds: If a merchant declines your order, they are required by policy to refund your food and delivery money within 30 minutes.

Safety & Navigation

  • Contacting Merchants: Long-press a merchant's name on the dashboard to view their WhatsApp number. Only use this for urgent coordination!
  • Verified Status: All merchants are physically verified by RUNHUB Admin for your safety.
  • History: Track your order status (Pending, Approved, Delivered, or Declined) in the History tab.
  • Ratings: Always rate your experience. Your feedback helps us keep the platform high-quality.
